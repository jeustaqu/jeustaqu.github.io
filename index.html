I am a second year Theater, Film, and Digital Productions major, with a concentration in Filmmaking, at University of California Riverside. I have always had a love for watching movies, and am fascinated by what goes on behind the scenes. Therefore, it is my aspiration to become part of the creative process. There is no greater joy than the thought of someone being inspired or having some significant emotional connection to something that I created, or at least had a hand in bringing to life.

I specifically want to work as an editor because I like figuring out how to piece together shots and scenes so that the story is coherent and expresses meaning. It's like a puzzle, and puzzles are fun for me. I have some experience with roles that require being on set and even had the opportunity to act as director, but those moments just enforced the notion that being an editor is my calling. I much prefer the leisure of sitting down and not having to worry about expensive equipment, besides my computer.

My other interests lie in creating storyboards and animatics so I am well-versed in figuring out how stories should be framed and structured. Drawing is an important skill for me, something that aids in my creativity and now helps me better understand how a story should take shape.
